How to Play 3-5-8 (Sergeant Major) 🃏

👥 Players: 3 players, standard 52-card deck.

🎲 Deal: Each gets 16 cards; the dealer picks up and discards 4 from the kitty.

🎯 Roles:
  • Dealer needs 8 tricks
  • Second player needs 5 tricks
  • Third player needs 3 tricks

🃏 Play: Dealer calls trump. Play tricks, follow suit if possible.

🧮 Scoring: Each trick over quota is +1, under is -1. Totals update after each round.

♻️ Card Exchange: Winners of extra tricks give lowest cards to those under, receive their highest.

🔄 Rotate: Roles and dealer rotate each round.
